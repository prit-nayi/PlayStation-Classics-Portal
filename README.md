## 🚀 **Getting Started**

### **Quick Start**
1. **🎮 Open the Hub**: Navigate to the PlayStation Hub
2. **🎯 Choose Your Game**: Select from our 4 classic games
3. **🎲 Start Playing**: Click "Play Now" on any game
4. **🏆 Enjoy**: Experience classic gaming with modern features!

### **🎮 How to Play Each Game**

#### **Simon Game**
```
1. Click "START GAME" to begin
2. Watch and listen to the pattern sequence
3. Click the buttons in the same order
4. Each level adds one more step to the sequence
5. Reach level 20 to win!
```

#### **Rock Paper Scissors**
```
1. Choose your weapon: 🪨 Rock, 📄 Paper, or ✂️ Scissors
2. Watch the AI make its choice
3. See who wins based on classic rules
4. Track your wins, losses, and ties
5. Try auto-play to watch AI vs AI battles!
```

#### **Tic Tac Toe**
```
1. You play as X, AI plays as O
2. Click any empty square to place your X
3. AI will respond with its move
4. Get 3 X's in a row to win
5. Adjust difficulty for different challenges
```

#### **Battleship**
```
1. Place your 5 ships on the board
2. Click "FIRE!" to attack enemy coordinates
3. Red = Hit, Gray = Miss
4. Sink all enemy ships to win
5. Use the battle log to track your strategy
```
