How to Play

Game Rules:

  • Players take turns placing X and O on the 3x3 grid
  • Get 3 of your symbols in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win
  • If all squares are filled without a winner, it's a tie
  • You play as X, AI plays as O

Controls & Buttons:

Grid Squares Click any empty square to place your X
NEW GAME Start a fresh game and reset the board
DIFFICULTY Switch between Easy, Medium, and Hard AI
AUTO PLAY Watch AI vs AI battles automatically

Difficulty Levels:

Easy AI makes random moves - good for beginners
Medium AI mixes smart and random moves - balanced challenge
Hard AI uses perfect strategy - nearly unbeatable

Tips for Victory:

  • Try to control the center square - it's the most strategic position
  • Look for opportunities to create multiple winning paths
  • Block your opponent when they're close to winning
  • Practice against different difficulty levels to improve
