How to Play

Game Rules:

  • Place your 5 ships on your board during setup phase
  • Take turns firing at enemy coordinates to find and sink their ships
  • Hit = Red square (you hit a ship)
  • Miss = Gray square (no ship at that location)
  • Sink all enemy ships to win the battle

Your Fleet:

Carrier (5 squares) Largest ship - place horizontally or vertically
Battleship (4 squares) Powerful warship - strategic placement key
Cruiser (3 squares) Medium vessel - good for defense
Submarine (3 squares) Stealth vessel - hard to find
Destroyer (2 squares) Small but fast - perfect for gaps

Controls & Buttons:

Ship Selection Click a ship to select it for placement
ROTATE SHIP Change ship orientation (horizontal/vertical)
RANDOM PLACEMENT Automatically place all ships randomly
FIRE! Click on enemy board to attack coordinates
NEW GAME Start a fresh battle with new ship placement
DIFFICULTY Adjust AI intelligence (Easy/Medium/Hard)

Strategic Tips:

  • Spread your ships out to avoid easy detection
  • Use the edges and corners for smaller ships
  • Track your hits to find ship patterns
  • Focus on areas where you've already hit ships
  • Don't cluster all ships in one area
